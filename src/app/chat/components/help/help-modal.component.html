How to Use Prate
To begin searching for a match, simply click 'FIND A MATCH'. Prate will open a matching dialog and begin looking for your match. The matching works by looking through your selected interests and finding other users with the same interests. If no other users have been found matching your interests, then you will be matched with the first available person.
Basic Navigation
To open up options and information dialogs, click the icons on your user information panel. The cog icon will open your user settings, the group icon will open your friends list, the question mark icon will open help, the bug icon will open a popup to send a bug report, and the X icon will log you out and return you to the home page. Clicking the Prate icon at any time will also return you to the home page without logging you out. On mobile these will just be named links inside a dropdown after tapping your user avatar.
Once matched with a user, you can simply begin chatting with him or her by entering text in the bottom textbox and hitting enter. You may leave your match at any time by clicking the 'LEAVE CHAT' button.
Gaining Experience
Prate awards experience points based on every 10 seconds spent matched. Experience points are awarded after either user disconnects. Users who are inactive for 10 minutes or longer will be automatically disconnected. Once your experience bar is filled, you will be rewarded and increase in level (see Badges).
Customization
Prate reflects most of your settings to your matches so you can be unique while chatting. Please see Customize for more details.
HTML Injection
Prate currently allows basic HTML injection to further style messages. Simply wrap your text in <></> to apply a style. Example: <h1>This will be large text</h1>. Currently supported tags include: H1-6, I, B, U, and Strong. Any malicious code or fake links will be striped from your message.
Hyperlinks & Images
To create a link within your message just begin your link with http:// or https:// followed by the link and Prate will automatically create a link to the URL for you. To show an image, paste in the link to the image (jpg, gif, png) and a preview will appear in chat.
Emojis
To insert an emoji, click the emoji button and select your emoji. It will automatically be applied to your message. You can also look at the emoji code and type it into messages to automatically insert the emoji.
YouTube Sharing
You can watch YouTube with your match by clicking on the YouTube icon and pasting in the link to the video then clicking the send invite button. An invite will be sent out to your match which they will have to accept, afterwhich the video will play inside the chat area. Video controls will appear at the bottom of the screen. Any video control will also be reflected to your partner so you may stay synced up. Clicking the 'X' will close the video for you and your partner.
Playing Games Together
You can play games with your match by clicking the game controller icon and selecting a game, afterwhich you will be prompted with instructions on getting an invite code. Paste the invite code into the textbox and click 'Send Invite' to invite your match to play a game with you. You can exit out of the game at any point by clicking the X at the top right, this will also close the game for your match.

Opening User Settings
To open your user settings, click on the cog icon inside your user information panel at the top right (or 'User Settings' after tapping your avatar on mobile). This will display all edittable settings. Settings include name, avatar, font customization, layout, interests, and password.
Changing your Avatar
Changing your avatar is easily achieved by clicking your avatar image at the top of the settings popup. This will open a new popup where you can select your new avatar then set it by clicking 'Apply'.
Customizing your Font
All settings applied to your font will be seen by both your and your matches. To change your font style, click the dropdown under 'Font' and choose one from the selection. To change your font color, click the color bar under 'Font Color' and drag around the color spectrum until you are happy with your selected color then click 'Ok'. The same setting can be applied to your bubble color (text background) by clicking the color bar under 'Bubble Color'.
Selecting Interests
To change your interests, select which topics you are interested in chatting about by clicking the checkboxes beside that topic. You may also add custom interests by entering topics into the textbox and hitting Enter after each topic. Prate will try to match you with people of similar interests.
Setting Up your Bio
You can write a short description about yourself under the 'Bio' category. This will be displayed on your profile for your matches to see.
Custom Options
By default, avatars are shown inside bubbles during chatting. To turn this off click 'No' beside 'Show Avatars'. To customizing bubble positions there are two options: 'Alternate', which places your sent messages on the right side and your match's messages on the left side (more mobile focused), and 'Left', which places all messages on the left (more desktop focused). You may also turn your notification sounds off by toggling the 'Sound' option. The 'Enforce Interest' option will force matching to search until someone with one of your interests is found. The 'Theme' option allows you to toggle between a light or dark website color theme.
Changing your Password
To change your password, click 'Change Password' located towards the bottom of the settings popup. Two textboxes will appear where you may enter your new password and old password for confirmation.
Applying your Settings
Remember to always click 'Apply' after changing values or your settings will not take effect.
