Why Prate?
Prate is free chat application for people who want to be matched with others based on their topic of interest. We enable our visitors to connect with those they wouldn't otherwise meet in the real world. Our goal is to encourage our users to build short or even long-lasting friendships through sharing experiences, values, and knowledge.

Interest Based Matching
Users will be matched with someone they can relate to, or you can just match randomly to chat with anyone! Not liking your match? You can leave your chat anytime and match with a another user. Our services allow for easy, seamless chatting across multiple platforms.

Prate doesn't discriminate!
Customization
Customize several aspects of your chatting experience. Change your display name, avatar, text color, bubble color, and font face easily with our user settings management. We even allow custom chat layout options. It's easy to be an individual on Prate and separate yourself from the rest.
Progression
Using Prate's leveling progression system, you can easily rank up and earn rewards to show off to your matches. Your chatting experience is reflected to other users so they know you know what you're doing. All of your chatting experience means something, and your time is never wasted.
